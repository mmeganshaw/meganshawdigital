Websites are generally either one of two things to those who know little about them:

1. Difficult to create and/or
2. Expensive

These two adjectives stem from the fact that not only are websites important, but they have always had a high barrier to entry for many non-tech savvy businesses.

We are in the midst of the digital age. Most business is done involving a computer, and most of your employees and customers will gauge you off of your website.

I love transforming websites. It amazes me how legitimate a website can make a brand that’s never even made a sale, and I love being a part of that journey. I also love seeing how employees light up when they can show off their employers new website to friends and family. It’s something everyone can be proud of.
