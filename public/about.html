If you would have told me when I first began my career in marketing, that it would become as varied as it has, I wouldn't have believed you (here's my LinkedIn, for reference).

What I've found as I've navigated the waters of the business world, is that each business is different. Each business has unique needs, and with unique needs, come unique skillsets required to fulfill those needs.

The reason I have experience in so many different types of marketing is not because I had the intention of skipping from one skill to the next, but moreso the intent to solve problems. I enjoy using the skillsets I have curated to come up with a hypothesis to a problem and then testing it. While many outside the field of marketing view it as aesthetic and nothing more, those deep within the field understand that it is an exact science: a science solved with just the right set of persuasive aesthetic and content.

And while there is certainly a technical skills side to what I do, I'm always interested in expanding that technical repetoire. If there's a problem you believe needs solved, I'm happy to work through it with you. Don't hesitate to send me a message.

- Megan Shaw
