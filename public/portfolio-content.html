The secret to finding what content best engages your audience is testing. In order to understand what your audience is going to engage with, you’re going to need to spoon feed it to them. Many times, and sometimes over and over again until you get it right.

Think goldilocks, except content, and there are way more than 3 bears.

By developing a hypothesis as to what you think is going to work with your audience, testing it, and repeating that process time and time again, you can develop a content strategy that will not only engage your audience, but dramatically lift your followership.
